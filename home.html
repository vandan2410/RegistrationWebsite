What's so special about your XenIOT 2.0 ?
XenIoT 2.0 is VIIT’s IoT Forum’s Flagship event. It will be for the beginners, intermediates and will help them to strengthen their command over the fields of automation and technology in general. XenIoT strives to support Tech enthusiasts to create a better world for tomorrow by guiding them throughout the journey and uplifting them.
How much do we charge ?
We have four types of entry for the event. First is early bird entry from 5th September to 7th September which costs Rs 25 per head. Second is the normal entry from 8th September to 16th September evening costing Rs 50 per head. Next is entry through Campus Ambassador for which early bird entry costs Rs 20 per head and normal entry Rs 40 per head. Lastly, you can use referral codes of ACM, CSI, or Computer department students and the charge is Rs 20 per head.
Is it virtual or offline ?
The event is entirely online. We are missing DJ nights and tasty food just like you.
It is my first event? Can I participate ?
Of course you can! Experience does not matter till the time you have the zest to change the world. New comers are most welcome!
Can I participate individually ?
You can participate individually or in team of 2-4.
